Welcome to TAG's Digital Realm. Greetings to both our newcomers and our seasoned TAG voyagers: your presence here is both acknowledged and valued.

We've tuned into your feedback, engaged with many of you on Discord, on socials, and face-to-face. Your voices remain the cornerstone of our evolution.

Many TAG supporters including the TAG Discord Community have been requesting an Anytime Access, "Economy" Grading option--we are excited to report it is finally here. Beginning today, customers may order a 10-40 card grading package at only $19 per card. The "Economy" tier turnaround will be 30 Business Days, and the "Express" $25 option has been upgraded to only 15 Business Days. TAG is also pleased to announce a new "Premier" service at $75 per card for a quick 3-5 day turnaround time.

You can check out more of the pricing details here. (Don't worry, this will open in a new page/tab and will not lose your spot in line!)

The increasing demand for TAG slabs has driven us to innovate, without compromising the integrity of our operation. We're committed to honoring every stated turnaround time. Our goal is to provide everyone keen on getting cards TAG'd with an equal opportunity to do so-- this is why the Drop Queue's are completely randomized. But we've realized: the more cards we accept per drop order, the more enthusiasts we have to turn away. So, in an effort to streamline operations and democratize access, we've set a card submission limit of 10 cards per Drop. This measure ensures a wider array of users can experience the unparalleled transparency of TAG at our most competitive rates.

To the future of grading, the love of cards, and the preservation of all collectibles!
